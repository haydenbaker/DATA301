\section{Data Collection and
Cleaning}\label{data-collection-and-cleaning}}

    \textbf{Research Question: What factors effect a country's proportion of
women in high ranking positions?}

How does women involvement in government predict prevalence of women in
the private sector? We hypothesized that more women in government will
mean more women in the country's top jobs. Female legislators lead the
country to increase laws concerning maternity leave, parental rights,
contreception, divorce laws, etc. We believe these aspects would
contribute to women being able to work full time and be able to hold
management positions.

    We used many separate data sets and wanted to combine all of these data
sets by country name. To address the problem of different sources using
different styles and notaion to label the same country, we created two
functions to help us combine data sets by country names. We also always
double checked that the functions correcly map the country names. If
necesary, we also hard coded any differences in country names that the
function did not detect.
